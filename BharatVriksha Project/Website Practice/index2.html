About Us

The Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most famous monuments in the world, renowned for its stunning beauty and historical significance. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal, the Taj Mahal is often referred to as a symbol of eternal love.
